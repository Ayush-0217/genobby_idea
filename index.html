The problem with today's apps

You switch your emotion every
60 seconds watching short videos.

That's not entertainment — that's rewiring. Short-form content resets your emotional state constantly. You never settle. You never go deep. You consume everything and retain nothing. Genobby was built as the antidote.

What is Genobby

A platform with intentional rules,
not accidental ones.

🖼️
Images only
Share your hobby through photos. A painting, a garden, handmade jewelry — let visuals speak.
✍️
Writing only
Words that go beyond captions. Describe your process, your journey, what you learned.
🚫
No videos allowed
No reels. No shorts. No autoplay. This is a deliberate, permanent rule — not a roadmap item.
🚫
No audio content
Pure reading and viewing. A quieter experience your mind can actually absorb and keep.

What Genobby does

AI that helps you grow,
not just scroll.

01

Discover your hobbies AI

Not sure what you're into? Genobby's AI asks you the right questions and helps figure out where your genuine interests lie — not based on ad revenue, but on you.

02

Go deeper into what you love AI

Once you post about a hobby, the AI brings in knowledge, context, and resources to help you understand it better. Like having a knowledgeable friend who's as passionate as you are.

03

Personalised next post suggestions AI

Based on your previous posts, Genobby suggests ideas for your next one — encouraging you to keep exploring, not just repeat yourself.

04

Real appreciation, no vanity metrics

No follower counts designed to make you feel small. Genobby is about community around shared interests — not social competition or numbers.

Meet Shaa

Your personal hobby AI — always in your corner.

Stuck on what to post next? Not sure where to start with a new hobby? Shaa is Genobby's built-in AI that listens, guides, and helps you get unstuck — without judgment.

Ask Shaa
I want to get into sketching but I don't know where to start 😅
That's a great place to begin! Start with just 10 minutes a day — draw basic shapes from observation. No eraser allowed. Here's a perfect first post idea for you: sketch 3 objects on your desk right now 🎨
